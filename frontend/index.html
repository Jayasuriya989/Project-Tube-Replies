About TubeReplies

TubeReplies is a web application built for YouTube content creators. It uses the YouTube Data API v3 to help creators manage and respond to comments on their videos more efficiently with the help of AI.

What Data We Access

After you sign in with Google, we access your YouTube channel information, your uploaded videos, and the public comments on those videos â€” solely to display them inside the app dashboard.

How We Use It

Your comment data is sent to Google Gemini AI to generate suggested reply drafts. No data is sold or shared with third parties. Replies are only posted to YouTube when you explicitly click "Post Reply".

Your Privacy

We do not permanently store your YouTube content or credentials. Your access token is held only for your active session. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.
