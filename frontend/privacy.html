Privacy Policy for Tube Replies

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tube Replies. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our web application to manage and reply to your YouTube comments.

2. Information We Collect

When you log in to Tube Replies using your Google Account, we access the following information via the YouTube Data API v3:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • YouTube Data: Details about your YouTube channel, videos, and comments on your videos.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data obtained from the YouTube API strictly to provide the features of the application, including:

  • Displaying your channel's videos and their respective comments.
  • Analyzing and clustering comments to suggest AI-generated replies.
  • Posting replies to YouTube comments on your behalf, but only when you explicitly click the "Reply" button.

We do not use your YouTube data for targeted advertising or sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not persistently store your YouTube comments or videos on our servers. The data is fetched in real-time when you use the app. Your authentication tokens are stored securely and are only used for the duration of your session.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Tube Replies's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Managing Your Data

You can revoke Tube Replies's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting the Google Security Settings page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our website.
